FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we get most often.

How do I post a job on Stackly?
Create a free recruiter account, complete your company profile, then click "Post a Job" in your dashboard. Your listing goes live instantly after a quick review.
Is Stackly free for job seekers?
Yes — browsing, applying, and saving jobs is completely free for candidates. We only charge employers for premium listings and advanced hiring tools.
How long does it take to get a response?
Our support team typically responds within one business day. For urgent technical issues, use the live chat widget in the bottom-right corner for faster assistance.
Can I delete my account and data?
Yes. Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. All your personal data is permanently removed within 30 days in compliance with GDPR and CCPA regulations.
Do you support international job listings?
Absolutely. Stackly lists roles in 80+ countries with currency and timezone localisation. Search by country or filter for "International" and "Visa Sponsored" roles.
How do I report a suspicious listing?
Click the flag icon on any job card to report it. Our trust and safety team reviews all reports within 24 hours and removes fraudulent listings immediately.
